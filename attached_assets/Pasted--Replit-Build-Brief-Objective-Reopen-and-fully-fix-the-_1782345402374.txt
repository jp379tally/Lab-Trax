### Debug Analysis

The repeated failure means prior fixes probably addressed only one symptom, likely the visible selection behavior, while leaving persistence or mobile refresh broken.

The dangerous clue is: **the app reports success but the dashboard/HUD does not update**. That means one of two things is happening:

1. The backend is not actually updating the case location/status, but the frontend still shows success.
2. The backend is updating correctly, but the mobile dashboard/HUD is reading stale cached data or a different field.

Either way, the success state is currently untrustworthy. The fix needs to prove the selected destination reaches the backend, the backend updates the correct case field, and the mobile HUD refetches the updated record.
