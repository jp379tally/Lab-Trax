# Task — New User Onboarding, Trial, and Subscription Flow

## What & Why

LabTrax is changing rapidly. New users need to understand:

* The platform is actively evolving.
* New features and improvements are released frequently.
* Their first month is free.
* After the trial ends, billing begins automatically (or they are prompted to subscribe, depending on your final billing model).
* Subscription payments must be tied directly to your LabTrax account so revenue flows to SDR/LabTrax and not into a disconnected payment system.

# Subscription & Billing Integration

## Ownership Requirement

Payments must belong to:

```text
SDR / LabTrax
```

not Replit.

Replit is only hosting the application.

Revenue should flow into your own payment processor account.

Recommended:

```text
Stripe
```

or equivalent subscription platform.

---

# Future-Proof Messaging

Add a section on signup:

> LabTrax is an actively developed platform. New features and improvements are released frequently. Your feedback directly influences future development.

This sets expectations correctly and turns the rapid pace of development into a selling point rather than a surprise.

---

One thing I would add: don't tell users they should expect **daily** updates. Internally you may deploy daily, but from a customer perspective it's better to promise:

> "Frequent feature updates and continuous improvements"

and then exceed expectations. Promising daily updates can create support expectations if a week goes by without a visible release.
