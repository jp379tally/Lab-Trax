My assessment of #1473:

### What it gets right

* Treats the desktop/web workflows as the source of truth.
* Eliminates the dual-data-model problem instead of adding more sync layers.
* Moves mobile to the same canonical API contracts.
* Pushes business logic back to the server/API layer.

### What I would change before approving implementation

#### 1. Don't promise "no local state" for offline recovery

There's a contradiction in the document.

Early on it says:

> No AsyncStorage case/invoice/photo state.

But acceptance criterion #10 says:

> Mutations queued during 60 s airplane mode flush successfully on reconnect. 

Those two statements conflict.

You need to decide:

**Option A (recommended):**

* Online-first.
* No offline mutation queue.
* If offline, show an error and don't save.

**Option B:**

* Keep a real mutation queue.
* Persist it locally.
* Explicitly design and test it.

Trying to do both is how you got into this situation originally.

---

#### 2. Rebuild Case Detail before Scan

I would change the order:

Current:

1. Foundation
2. Cases
3. Scan

I'd do:

1. Foundation
2. Cases list
3. Case detail
4. Photo upload/view
5. Scan / AI Reader
6. Invoices

Reason:
If Case Detail isn't canonical first, Scan has nowhere trustworthy to send users after creating a case.
