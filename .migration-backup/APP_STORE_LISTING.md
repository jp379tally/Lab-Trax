## Description

LabTrax is the all-in-one dental laboratory management app built for modern labs and the providers they serve. Track cases from intake to shipment, manage clients and invoicing, and keep your entire team connected — all from your iPhone or iPad.

**For Dental Labs**

- Track every case through your production workflow — Intake, Design, Scan, Mill, Porcelain, QC, and Shipping — with a visual station board that keeps your team in sync.
- Scan dental prescriptions with your camera and let AI automatically extract patient info, tooth numbers, materials, and shade — no manual entry required.
- Scan and assign barcodes to cases for fast location and batch processing across your lab floor.
- Manage your full client list with tiered pricing, custom price lists, and account-level detail.
- Generate invoices automatically when cases are created, send monthly billing statements, and track open balances and payment status in real time.
- Monitor revenue with built-in sales analytics — daily, month-to-date, year-to-date, or custom date ranges — with breakdowns by material and top clients.
- Manage inventory with stock tracking, categories, and low-stock alerts.
- Use the built-in document scanner to capture, crop, and save prescriptions and lab slips as clean, professional PDFs.
- Communicate directly with providers through in-app messaging with read receipts and unread indicators.
- Send automated courtesy texts to providers when cases are delayed, and negotiate updated delivery dates right from the case detail screen.

**For Dental Providers**

- Submit cases to your lab and track their status in real time — see exactly where your case is in the production process.
- Use AI-powered Smile Preview to show patients what their smile could look like with whitening, symmetry correction, or both — right from your chair.
- View invoices, case history, and communicate with your lab without phone calls or emails.
- Receive real-time notifications when cases move through stations or when your lab needs to reach you.

**Built for Security**

- HIPAA-conscious design with data isolation between labs and practices.
- Biometric lock screen with Face ID support to protect sensitive patient data.
- Automatic inactivity timeout locks the app when you step away.
- Secure account management with password recovery via email.

**Admin Control Center**

- Full admin dashboard with client management, user roles, tier pricing, shipping accounts, and integrations.
- Deactivate or delete clients with built-in safeguards — open invoices are archived and excluded from financial reporting automatically.
- Multi-user lab support — invite team members to join your lab and share cases across devices.

Whether you run a two-person bench lab or manage a multi-location operation, LabTrax gives you the tools to stay organized, communicate clearly, and grow your business.
